ECU connectors

90-93 and 94-95 needs the 48 pin connector (Tyco 174917-7)

Tyco 174917-7 (48-pin connector)fits 90-95 only
View at Picasa
 

 

The 64 pin connector (Tyco 174518-7) and
the 76 pin connector (Tyco 174915-6) also fit (see below).

 

96-97 and 99-00 needs the 64 pin connector (Tyco 174518-7)

Tyco 174518-7 (64-pin connector) needed on 99-00 but fits anything from '90-'00
View at Picasa
 

 

 

 

90-00 can also use the larger 76 pin connector (Tyco 174915-6).

174915-6 (76-pin connector) fits anything from 90-00
View at Picasa
 

 

 

 

01-05 needs the 72 pin connector (Tyco 1123038-2), no other connector fits.

Tyco 1123038-2 (72-pin connector), fits 01-05 only
View at Picasa
 

 

 

 

 

90-00 connectors:
(1) 64 Pin Male connector (174518-7)
(1) 26 Pin Female connector (174516-6)
(1) 22 Pin Female connector (174515-6)
(32) .040″ Contacts (175061-1)
(16) .070″ Contacts (173631-6)

96-97 add:
(16) .040″ Contacts (175061-1)
(1) 16 Pin Female connector (174514-6)

2001 Connectors:
353027-1 (17 pos)
353029-1 (24 pos)
353031-1 (31 pos)

Pins:
316836-1 (72 minimum required)
